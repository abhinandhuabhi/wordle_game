WORDLE





How to play?

Guess the Wordle in 6 tries

               Each guess should be a valid 5-letter word.
                                                                   The colour of the tile changes to show how close you are to guess your word.
                                                                  If a character of the word appears in the correct position then it turns green.
                                                                  If a character of the word appears in the wrong position then it turns yellow.
                                                If a character not present in the word appears then it turns grey
